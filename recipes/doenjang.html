Doenjang Jjigae

Description

Doenjang Jjigae is one of the most popular staple dishes in korean cuisine. It is a stew made from a base of doenjang, fermented soybean paste.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium potato, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 medium onion, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 small zucchini, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 green Korean chili pepper, stemmed and chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 large shrimp, shelled, deveined, washed and coarsely chopped
  • 2 and 1/4 cups water
  • 7 dried anchovies, gutted
  • 5 Tbsp fermented soybean paste (Doenjang)
  • 6 ounces medium-firm tofu, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Combine the potato, onion, zucchini, chili pepper, garlic and shrimp in a 1 and 1/2 quart heavy pot.
  2. Wrap dried anchovies in cheesecloth, and put into pot with other ingredients. Add water and cover.
  3. Cook over medium-high heat for 15 minutes until boiling.
  4. Stir in soybean paste, mixing well. Cover and cook for 20 minutes over medium heat.
  5. Add tofu and cook for another 3 minutes. Discard anchovy pouch.
  6. Sprinkle with green onions and serve with rice.
